Dinolotl: New Pet Monthly Challenge Submission!

Hi Guys!

This is my monthly challenge submission! I designed a new pet for our #30nitesOfCode. For my design I created the Dinolotl! Half dinosaur. half axolotl. I choose an axolotl a my main source of inspo as the species is very dear to me!
The axolotl originates from the Central Mexico, and are native to its freshwater lakes, Lake Xochimilco and Lake Chalco. Aside from my personal affinity for them, a big reason I choose the axolotl was to bring awareness to the species, as it is unfortunately under direct threat of extinction as a result of human intervention, as of January 2024 - there are only an estimated 50 - 1000 left in the wild.
